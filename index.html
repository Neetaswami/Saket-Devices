Saket Devices Pvt Ltd

We are the exclusive promoter and support partner for Sanken Electric's power semiconductor products in India, specializing in automotive, consumer electronics, and white goods applications. Our offerings include a diverse range of products such as motor drivers, power management ICs, and discrete semiconductors, all designed to enhance power conversion efficiency in various sectors. By leveraging Sanken Electric's advanced technologies, we aim to provide innovative solutions that meet the evolving needs of the Indian market.
