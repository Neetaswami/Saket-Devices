About Us

Leading the semiconductor revolution in India

Saket Devices Pvt Ltd

We are the exclusive promoter and support partner for Sanken Electric's power semiconductor products in India, specializing in automotive, consumer electronics, and white goods applications. Our offerings include a diverse range of products such as motor drivers, power management ICs, and discrete semiconductors, all designed to enhance power conversion efficiency in various sectors.

By leveraging Sanken Electric's advanced technologies, we aim to provide innovative solutions that meet the evolving needs of the Indian market.

Sanken Electric Co., Ltd

A global leader in power semiconductor technology since 1946, Sanken Electric specializes in intelligent power modules, motor drivers, and advanced power management solutions. With over 75 years of innovation, Sanken has established itself as a top-tier semiconductor manufacturer.
